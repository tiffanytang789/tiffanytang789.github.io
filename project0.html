Project 0 — Becoming Friends with Your Camera

Part 1. Selfie: The Wrong Way vs. The Right Way

The image distortion comes from wide angle capture of camera, which stretches the edge. As we move our face further from camera, our face is further from frame edge, resulting in less distortion.

Part 2. Architectural Perspective Compression

When we are close to the objects, distance between different things in the frame are more obvious since our distance is relatively short from all those objects. However, as we move further away, our distance to the objects are much greater than the relative distances between different objects in the frame, making those small distances less obvious and look flattened.
